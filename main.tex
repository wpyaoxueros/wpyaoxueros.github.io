    \end{minipage}
\end{twocolentry}

\vspace{0.10 cm}
\begin{onecolentry}
    \begin{highlights}
        \item \textbf{Benchmark Construction:} Proposed \textbf{CEBench}, a cross-embodiment benchmark spanning simulation and real-world robotic tasks with domain randomization, including single-arm, bimanual, and mobile manipulation.

        \item \textbf{Lightweight Model Design:} Developed \textbf{LightVLA}, a lightweight, pretraining-free vision-language-action model integrating multi-view perception, proprioceptive tokenization, and hybrid action chunking.

        \item \textbf{Training Paradigm Innovation:} Introduced a two-stage \textbf{post-training + fine-tuning} strategy enabling small models to match or surpass larger ones without costly large-scale pretraining.

        \item \textbf{Unified Action Space:} Designed a hybrid discrete–continuous action representation unifying navigation and manipulation for cross-embodiment control.

        \item \textbf{Experimental Results:} Achieved performance comparable to or exceeding 7B-scale models and established the \textbf{first end-to-end VLA} capable of real-world mobile manipulation.
    \end{highlights}
\end{onecolentry}

% ---------- Project 4 ----------
\begin{twocolentry}{}
    \begin{minipage}[t]{\textwidth}
        \justifying
        \textbf{RoboMemArena: A Keyframe-Annotated Benchmark for Memory-Dependent Robotic Manipulation}
    \end{minipage}
\end{twocolentry}

\vspace{0.10 cm}
\begin{onecolentry}
    \begin{highlights}
        \item Built \textbf{RoboMemArena}, a long-horizon manipulation benchmark with \textbf{26 tasks} and \textbf{100 demonstrations per task} (\textbf{2,600} trajectories total), covering transferring, occlusion, counting, and sequential execution settings.
        \item Developed a simulation-first automatic annotation pipeline with VLM-driven decomposition, AnyGrasp-based execution, and keyframe extraction, producing \textbf{14,900} subtask-level short trajectories with native keyframe supervision.
        \item Designed memory-intensive evaluation protocol with average trajectory length of \textbf{1,110} steps and \textbf{76.3\%} memory-dependent subtasks, enabling systematic assessment of non-Markovian robotic decision making.
        \item Proposed \textbf{PrediMem}, a hierarchical VLM--VLA baseline with a predictive coding head that improves keyframe sensitivity during training and is removed at inference for \textbf{zero extra test-time cost}.
        \item PrediMem achieved the strongest overall performance on RoboMemArena, reaching top results on both task success rate (TSR) and cumulative success rate (CSR) among compared baselines.
    \end{highlights}
\end{onecolentry}
