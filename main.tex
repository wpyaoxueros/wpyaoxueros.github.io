\section{Projects}

% ---------- Project 1 ----------
\begin{twocolentry}{}
    \begin{minipage}[t]{\textwidth}
        \justifying
        \textbf{RoboMemArena: A Keyframe-Annotated Benchmark for Memory-Dependent Robotic Manipulation}
    \end{minipage}
\end{twocolentry}

\vspace{0.10 cm}
\begin{onecolentry}
    \begin{highlights}
        \item Built \textbf{RoboMemArena}, a long-horizon manipulation benchmark with \textbf{26 tasks} and \textbf{100 demonstrations per task} (\textbf{2,600} trajectories total), covering transferring, occlusion, counting, and sequential execution settings.
        \item Developed a simulation-first automatic annotation pipeline with VLM-driven decomposition, AnyGrasp-based execution, and keyframe extraction, producing \textbf{14,900} subtask-level short trajectories with native keyframe supervision.
        \item Designed memory-intensive evaluation protocol with average trajectory length of \textbf{1,110} steps and \textbf{76.3\%} memory-dependent subtasks, enabling systematic assessment of non-Markovian robotic decision making.
        \item Proposed \textbf{PrediMem}, a hierarchical VLM--VLA baseline with a predictive coding head that improves keyframe sensitivity during training and is removed at inference for \textbf{zero extra test-time cost}.
        \item PrediMem achieved the strongest overall performance on RoboMemArena, reaching top results on both task success rate (TSR) and cumulative success rate (CSR) among compared baselines.
        \item Links: \href{https://robomemarena.github.io/}{Project Page}, \href{https://mp.weixin.qq.com/s/9puBzX48bsQeODE8DmRWUg}{Share}, \href{https://github.com/wpyaoxueros/RoboMemArena}{GitHub}.
    \end{highlights}
\end{onecolentry}
